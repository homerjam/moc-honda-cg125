Honda CG125 Enclosed Chain Guard

< How to remove and install the wheels in the Bike

< Sprockets

You only need to remove the Enclosed Chain Guard to remove rear wheel,
replace chain or check / replace sprockets.

Below is the plastic chain guard model, the metal chain guard model is similar.

The chain guard will remove without removing front engine / sprocket cover.


Remove the 4 screws 2 top and 2 bottom.


Remove screw at the back of chain guard.

You can now remove the entire chain guard (it's in two halves, one top and one bottom).


Back view of chain guard when off.


Back view of guard were the two halves clip together,
it's hard to see when you're putting guard back together from front.

Put top chain guard half on first and then fit bottom half.
